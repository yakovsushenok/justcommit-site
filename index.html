Commitments - How It Works

Learn the basics of creating and managing your fitness commitments

What is a commitment? ‚ñº
A commitment is a personal challenge where you stake real money to motivate yourself to achieve a specific fitness goal. You set a goal (like walking 10,000 steps daily), choose how much money to stake, and set a time period. If you achieve your goal, you keep your stake and may earn from the reward pool. If you do not meet your goal, your stake is contributed to the pool shared with successful participants.
How do I create a commitment? ‚ñº
Tap the '+' button on the Commitments tab, then:

1. Choose your activity type (steps, calories, distance, etc.)
2. Set your goal amount and time period
3. Choose how much money to stake
4. Select 'Practice Mode' for a trial run, or proceed to stake real money
5. Review your commitment details
6. Sign with your finger to confirm

You'll need a valid payment method on file before creating staked commitments.

Practice Mode lets you try commitments without staking money. It's perfect for:

‚Ä¢ Testing if your goals are realistic
‚Ä¢ Getting familiar with how the app tracks your progress
‚Ä¢ Understanding how commitments work before staking money

Practice commitments simulate the full experience with no financial risk or reward.
What happens when I sign a commitment? ‚ñº
When you sign a commitment:

1. Your payment method is verified for the staked amount
2. The commitment becomes active
3. The app begins tracking your progress automatically
4. You can monitor your progress in real-time
5. At the deadline, the app automatically evaluates if you succeeded

Your signature serves as digital consent to participate in a voluntary goal challenge using the amount you've staked.
What happens if I don't meet my goal? ‚ñº
If you do not meet your goal, your staked amount is contributed to a shared reward pool distributed among users who successfully completed their commitments. This system helps reinforce motivation through meaningful commitment, while also benefiting the broader user community.
Is staking money required to use the app? ‚ñº
No. Staking is completely optional. Users can choose Practice Mode to participate in all commitment features without any financial involvement. The core features of goal setting and progress tracking are accessible to all users regardless of financial participation.
